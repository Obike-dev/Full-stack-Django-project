About us

Have a business or brand that deals with selling shoes? Don't have enough capital to start up your website?. Don't worry Postyourshoes has you covered. Postyourshoes is a website that deals with the posting of shoe products for businesses, organizations or individuals so anyone around the world can see and patronize your business. When we say posting, what that simply means is putting your shoe brand or business out to the world.
You don't have to worry about any kind of payments as this is totally free and available to anyone around the world. We won't and will never ask for your card details.
We can also help you locate your favorite shoes. The more users posts their shoe products, the more it is easier for you to find that shoe you saw in your various stores.
There is also no limit to the number of shoe products that can be posted here on our website. This might be the turning point in your website why don't you join us right now. We'll be happy to have you as one of our most appreciated users.
NOTE shoes can not be sold from this website as this is just a website to help put your business out to the world.

Posting shoes

Posting shoes is a very simple procedure. All you really need to do is signup using our form or using your google accounts. If you already have an account, you can simply Login and continue. Note you won't be able to post any shoes if you are not signed in or you don't have an account. This is to avoid unauthorized access to your shoe products and to have each user relationships with shoes each particular shoe they have posted or removed.
After authenticating your password you can either tap the link on the navigation bar or go to your user profile account and tap on the ADD SHOE link there. You will be prompted to a form which you are going to fill. Note, not all fields are required in the form but it is much better to fill the whole form as this will enhance better experince with other users when they look at the details of your shoe. The fields in the form are fields like the name of the shoe, the shoe brand, some images which are gotten from the user filling the form, price you would like to sell the shoe, your brand social media accounts. E.g Instagram,Twitter,Facebook accounts etc.
After filling the form you would see the shoe appear on your profile page and in the category you selected when filling the form. You can then click on the image to see the full detail of the shoe you just created.

Edit shoe

If you want to make changes to a shoe you have already posted all you need to do is go to your profile page and click on the EDIT SHOE LINK and you will be directed to the form you used to add a shoe but this time the form will already be filled with the previous information you have already filled about that shoe.
You can also delete a shoe. Go to the EDIT SHOE link and scroll down. There is a delete shoe button there and it will handle the removal of that shoe model. Note every information about that shoe will be deleteed.The images,social media accounts etc. Think very well before deleting a shoe as this process cannot be restored.

User profile

Your user profile is where you can access and edit all your related data. Your profile is automatically created for you after signing up. You can access your profile by clicking on the user profile icon in the navigation bar.
You can edit your profile by clicking on the edit profile button in your user profile page. Data like your profile image, email, username, changing your password, deleting your account etc. can be edited by you in your user profile. Logging out of your account can also be done from our profile.

Privacy policy

We don't give out any information you have filled in the form out to any user. We only require your email for resetting your password if needed and sending you updates on our website when needed. NOTE no information can be accessed by any user apart from you. That is why we don't ask for your phone number when filling out your form as we don't want unwanted user access using your phone numbers for malicious activities. Your profile can only be edited by you and no one else.
